---
title: "reallocarray(3)"
---

`reallocarray`  is a memory-overflow-safe systemcall that ensures data is
discarded before allocating new memory.

Details:

* [malloc(3) - OpenBSD manual pages](http://man.openbsd.org/reallocarray)
