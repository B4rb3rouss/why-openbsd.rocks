---
title: "mg(1)"
---

`mg` is a small, lightweight emacs clone thats in the base system. Sysadmins
that do not like `vi(1)` have an alternative to use.

Details:

* [mg(1) - OpenBSD manual pages](http://man.openbsd.org/OpenBSD-current/man1/mg.1)
